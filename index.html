WHY STUDY MBBS ABROAD?

It is a dream for students to study medicine and practice it someday. However, the education system in India results in less than half a number of seats of 10 times the applicants. Next to impossible to get into Government colleges in India and a huge budget barrier for Private colleges clubbed with low quality of education in Indian private medical colleges, MBBS ABROAD is the best possible option that the students consider. Low cost of education, High Quality of education & Global exposure turn out to be a win- win situation for the Indian students. There are many Top Medical Universities in various countries open for Indian students.

HOW TO SELECT THE RIGHT UNIVERSITY ABROAD

With hundreds of universities promoted by different agents in India, it is very confusing and difficult for the students to select the right university that will actually put their career on the right path. To select the right Medical university abroad, understand the following points:

● Select a 100% English Medium Program.
● It has to be a Government university, not a private one. ( Except USA & Carribean)
● Make sure the University is MCI approved
● Select a university which has a high MCI passing ratio.
● Follow Government & Embassy reports as they are the only authentic source.
● Do your own research very, very well.

PREPARE FOR YOUR PG DURING MBBS!

One of the biggest mistakes made by Indian students is that, they do not prepare and plan in time for their PG and by the time they realise that, its too late. The situation for PG seat in India is twice as difficult as MBBS. Without specialisation, an MBBS degree holds no value. You can take up USMLE, German language or MCI Online classes and prepare for your PG in USA or Germany or India well in advance that would assure a 100% full proof road map to a highly successful medical career and a chance to practice in different parts of the world as well.
